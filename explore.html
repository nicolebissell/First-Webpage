On this page I have an example of an Explore Task that I made in preparation for the AP Computer Science Principles Test. The Explore Task is an 8 hour project where you are required to make an artifact explaining a computing innovation and answer a series of written responses explaining your project. Your artifact must be created electronically. For the example below, I used video chatting as my computing innovation.
